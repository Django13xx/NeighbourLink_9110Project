Welcome to NeighbourLink, a company dedicated to environmental sustainability. Our innovative car-sharing platform connects drivers and riders, reducing carbon footprints while fostering community connections. Drivers share ride details and suggest petrol contributions, while riders choose rides and contribution amounts. Our transparent system ensures trust and once agreements are made, no additional requests are accepted. Join us in creating a greener future with NeighbourLink.
