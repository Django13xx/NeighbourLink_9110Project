What type of information we collect

  • Account information: includes first and last name, date of birth, gender, email, phone number, login username and password, address, profile picture, payment or banking information, user settings, income history, number of trips
  • Background check information: verification status, license number, profile pictures
  • Usage information: date, time of service use, location, payment amount, chat logs with chatbot, call logs

How we collect your information

  • When you sign up to our platform
  • When booking and using our carsharing services
  • When making payments
  • When communicating with drivers/riders
  • When communicating with the chatbot/customer service

Why we collect such personal information

  • To provide and operate our carsharing services
  • To provide riders and drivers with ongoing customer service and technical assistance
  • To promote services and deals to drivers and riders
  • To improve our services by aggregating and analyzing statistical data
  • To comply with any applicable laws and regulations

How we store, use, share and disclose your personal information

NeighbourLink is hosted on AWS. This platform allows us to provide carsharing services to riders and drivers. Your data may be stored in our databases and our applications.

How we communicate with site visitors

NeighbourLink may reach out to you for various matters, including resolving issues about your account, resolving a dispute, collecting fees owed, notifying you of updates, such as promotions. For these purposes, we may contact you via the website, email, phone, text message, or post.
